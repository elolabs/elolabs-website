Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

SubRadar is designed with privacy at its core. We do not collect, store, or share your personal data. All your subscription information stays on your device — always.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

SubRadar does not collect any personally identifiable information. Specifically, we do not collect:

  • Your name, email address, or any account credentials
  • Your subscription data, payment history, or spending information
  • Device identifiers or location data
  • Usage analytics or behavioral data

2. Data Storage

All data you enter in SubRadar — subscriptions, payment records, categories, settings — is stored exclusively on your device using local storage. No data is transmitted to our servers or any third-party servers.

SubRadar works fully offline. An internet connection is only used for two optional features: fetching live exchange rates (via a privacy-safe proxy) and in-app purchases (Pro subscription).

3. Exchange Rate Fetching

If you use the multi-currency feature, SubRadar may fetch current exchange rates through a Cloudflare Worker proxy. This request contains no personal information — only a request for currency conversion rates. Rates are cached on your device for 24 hours to minimize network requests.

4. In-App Purchases

SubRadar offers an optional Pro subscription via Google Play. Purchase transactions are handled entirely by Google Play and RevenueCat, subject to their respective privacy policies. We only receive confirmation of whether a subscription is active — no payment details are ever shared with us.

5. Notifications

SubRadar may send local notifications to remind you of upcoming subscription renewals or contract end dates. These notifications are generated and delivered entirely on your device — no data leaves your phone to make this work.

6. Backup & Export

The Backup & Export feature (Pro) allows you to export your subscription data as a JSON file. This export is initiated by you and shared via your device's native share sheet. We have no access to exported files.

7. Third-Party Services

SubRadar uses the following third-party services:

  • RevenueCat — subscription management. Their privacy policy applies to purchase flows.
  • Google Play — app distribution and payments.
  • Frankfurter API / Cloudflare — anonymous exchange rate data. No personal data is transmitted.

We do not use any advertising networks, analytics SDKs, or tracking tools.

8. Children's Privacy

SubRadar does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. Since we collect no personal data at all, the app is safe for users of all ages.

9. Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, the new version will be posted on this page with an updated date. We encourage you to review it periodically.

10. Contact

Questions about this privacy policy? Contact us at support@elolabs.app.
