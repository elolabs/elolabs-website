Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

ClearBudget is designed with privacy at its core. We do not collect, store, or share your personal data. All your financial information stays on your device — always.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

ClearBudget does not collect any personally identifiable information. Specifically, we do not collect:

  • Your name, email address, or any account credentials
  • Financial data, transaction history, or budget information
  • Device identifiers or location data
  • Usage analytics or behavioral data

2. Data Storage

All data you enter in ClearBudget — transactions, categories, budgets, goals — is stored exclusively on your device using local storage. No data is transmitted to our servers or any third-party servers.

ClearBudget works fully offline. An internet connection is only required for optional features such as in-app purchases (Pro subscription).

3. In-App Purchases

ClearBudget offers an optional Pro subscription via Google Play. Purchase transactions are handled entirely by Google Play and RevenueCat, subject to their respective privacy policies. We only receive confirmation of whether a subscription is active — no payment details are ever shared with us.

4. Backup & Export

The Backup & Export feature (Pro) allows you to export your data as a JSON or CSV file. This export is initiated by you and shared via your device's native share sheet. We have no access to exported files.

5. Third-Party Services

ClearBudget uses the following third-party services:

  • RevenueCat — subscription management. Their privacy policy applies to purchase flows.
  • Google Play — app distribution and payments.

We do not use any advertising networks, analytics SDKs, or tracking tools.

6. Children's Privacy

ClearBudget does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. Since we collect no data at all, the app is safe for users of all ages.

7. Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, the new version will be posted on this page with an updated date. We encourage you to review it periodically.

8. Contact

Questions about this privacy policy? Contact us at support@elolabs.app.
