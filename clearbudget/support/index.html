Frequently Asked Questions
Is ClearBudget really free?

Yes. The Free plan is free forever with no ads and no time limits. A Pro subscription unlocks advanced features like unlimited categories, charts, backup, and more.

Is my data private and secure?

Completely. ClearBudget stores all your data locally on your device. We never see it, never sync it to a server, and never share it with anyone. The app works fully offline.

How do I backup my data?

Backup & Restore is a Pro feature. Go to Settings → Data Management → Export Backup. You can export a full JSON backup or a CSV transaction list and save it anywhere you like.

How do I restore a purchase?

If you reinstalled the app or switched devices, go to Settings → ClearBudget Pro → tap "Restore Purchases". Your subscription will be restored using your Google Play account.

How do I cancel my Pro subscription?

You can cancel anytime through Google Play. Open Google Play → Profile → Payments & subscriptions → Subscriptions → ClearBudget Pro → Cancel.

The app is in the wrong language. How do I change it?

Go to Settings → Language and select your preferred language. ClearBudget supports 10 languages: English, Turkish, German, French, Spanish, Italian, Russian, Japanese, Chinese, and Korean.
