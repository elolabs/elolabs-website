Frequently Asked Questions
Is MarketSet free?

Yes. The free plan gives you unlimited shopping lists, a full product catalog, favorites (up to 6), and basic statistics. Pro unlocks unlimited favorites, advanced statistics, 7 extra themes, unlimited reminders, CSV export, and data backup.

Is my data private and secure?

Completely. MarketSet stores everything locally on your device. We never see your shopping lists, spending history, or any personal information. The app works fully offline.

How do I add products to a list?

Open the Catalog tab, select your list at the top, then browse categories or search for a product. Tap a product to add it, adjust the quantity with the +/− buttons, then tap "Add X Items" at the bottom. You can also type any product name to add it instantly even if it's not in the catalog.

How does shopping mode work?

Tap the cart badge on any list to enter shopping mode. Your items appear on the left — tap or swipe them to move to your cart on the right. If price tracking is on, you can add unit prices and see your running total. When done, tap "Finish Shopping" to save the trip.

How do I restore a Pro purchase?

Go to Settings → MarketSet Pro → tap "Restore Purchases". Your purchase will be restored using your Google Play account.

How do I cancel my Pro subscription?

You can cancel anytime through Google Play. Open Google Play → Profile → Payments & subscriptions → Subscriptions → MarketSet Pro → Cancel. You'll keep Pro access until the end of your billing period.

How do I back up my data?

Backup & Restore is a Pro feature. Go to Settings → Data Management → Backup Data. Your data is saved as a JSON file that you can store anywhere and restore later.

Can I share my shopping list?

Yes! In shopping mode, tap the share icon in the header. Your list will be formatted as a text message you can send via any app — WhatsApp, SMS, email, and so on.

The app is in the wrong language. How do I change it?

Go to Settings → Language and select your preferred language. MarketSet supports 28 languages.
