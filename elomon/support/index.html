Frequently Asked Questions
Is EloMon free?

Yes. EloMon is free to download and the core experience is completely free — create your monster, customize it, and export wallpapers and stickers with no time limits. A Pro pack unlocks additional premium backgrounds and outfits.

What does EloMon Pro include?

EloMon Pro is available as a one-time purchase — no subscriptions. You can buy the Background Pack (10 exclusive HD scenes), the Outfit Pack (10 premium outfits), or the Full Pack which includes both plus an ad-free experience.

How do I export my creation as a wallpaper?

Tap the ⬇ (Save) button on the right side of the canvas, then select "Wallpaper". Your creation will be exported as a full-screen PNG and shared via your device's share sheet — save it to your gallery and set it as your wallpaper.

How do I export a sticker?

Tap the ⬇ (Save) button and select "Sticker". Your monster will be exported as a 512×512 PNG. You can then share it via WhatsApp, Telegram, or any messaging app that supports image sharing.

How do I add text to my creation?

Tap the 🪄 (Text) button on the right icon bar. Type your message, choose a color and size, then tap Add. You can drag the text anywhere on the canvas before exporting.

How do I change the app language?

Tap the ⚙️ (Settings) button in the top right, then tap "Language". EloMon supports 9 languages: English, Turkish, Japanese, Korean, German, French, Chinese, Portuguese, and Spanish.

How do I restore my purchase?

If you reinstalled the app or switched devices, your purchase should restore automatically when you open the app while signed in to the same Google Play account. If it doesn't, please contact us at support@elolabs.app.

Is my data private?

Yes. EloMon does not collect or store any personal data. Your creations stay on your device. The free version shows ads via Google AdMob — purchasing the Full Pack removes all ads.
