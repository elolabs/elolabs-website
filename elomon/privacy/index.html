Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

EloMon is designed with your privacy in mind. We do not collect your personal data. Everything you create stays on your device.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

EloMon does not collect any personally identifiable information. Specifically, we do not collect:

  • Your name, email address, or any account credentials
  • Your creations, character designs, or exported images
  • Device identifiers or location data
  • Usage analytics or behavioral data

2. Data Storage

EloMon does not use any cloud storage or external servers. Your character creations and app preferences are stored exclusively on your device. No data is transmitted to our servers or any third-party servers.

3. In-App Purchases

EloMon offers optional one-time purchases (Background Pack, Outfit Pack, Full Pack) via Google Play, managed through RevenueCat. Purchase transactions are handled entirely by Google Play and RevenueCat, subject to their respective privacy policies. We only receive confirmation of whether a purchase is active — no payment details are ever shared with us.

4. Exported Content

When you export a wallpaper or sticker, the image is saved to your device's local storage or shared via your device's native share sheet. We have no access to exported files.

5. Third-Party Services

EloMon uses the following third-party services:

  • RevenueCat — in-app purchase management. Their privacy policy applies to purchase flows.
  • Google Play — app distribution and payments.

6. Children's Privacy

EloMon is designed to be safe and fun for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. Since we collect no personal data ourselves, the app is safe for young users.

7. Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, the new version will be posted on this page with an updated date. We encourage you to review it periodically.

8. Contact

Questions about this privacy policy? Contact us at support@elolabs.app
