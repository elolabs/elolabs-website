How It Works
Three steps to earning more.
1
Use the right card at the right time
Right after a card's statement date, FloatCard tells you to use it. This gives you the longest possible time before payment is due.
2
Keep your money in a money market fund
From your income date to the due date, your money earns daily interest in a PPF/money market fund instead of sitting idle.
3
Pay the full statement on the due date
Pay the complete balance on the last day. FloatCard calculates your net interest earnings after tax — showing you exactly how much you made.
